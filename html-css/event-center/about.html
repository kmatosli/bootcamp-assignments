Our Story

The Curious Itinerary

Built by a traveler who got tired of paying more for less.

The Founder

I Built the Trip
I Couldn't Find

I have done the budget bus tours. I have done the prestige trips. I have sat on the long transfers, watched evenings dissolve into aimless free time, and seen thoughtful travelers pay premium prices for experiences that never quite came together.

The Curious Itinerary is my answer to that frustration. I wanted a travel experience that was intellectually serious, socially warm, logistically efficient, and priced with honesty.

I’m a licensed travel agent, a data scientist and data engineer, and someone who completed the full coursework for a Spanish Literature degree. That combination means I can design journeys that are culturally rich, operationally tight, and transparent about what you are actually paying for.

No solo upcharge. No itinerary inflation. No paying extra just to be told something is “luxury.” Just better judgment, stronger structure, and more meaning per day.

Our Philosophy

What We Believe
About Travel

Context changes everything.

Standing in front of the Alhambra is one experience. Standing there understanding Nasrid power, Reconquista trauma, and Lorca’s obsession with Granada is another. We provide the context that transforms the site.

Community is the hidden luxury.

The best souvenir from a trip is rarely what you bought. It’s the people you met and the conversations you remember. We design for that on purpose.

Premium should mean discernment.

We believe premium travel should mean better sequencing, stronger guides, better hotel judgment, and less wasted time — not louder branding.
